# Privacy Policy

**YNAB Budget Alerts**
*Last updated: February 9, 2026*

## Overview

YNAB Budget Alerts is a private, household messaging service that sends automated personal finance notifications via SMS/MMS. This service is operated by an individual for personal and household use only. It is not a business, marketing platform, or commercial service.

## What Data We Collect

- **Phone numbers** of household members who have consented to receive transaction alerts
- **Bank transaction data** (merchant name, transaction amount, date, and last four digits of account number) received via automated SMS alerts from Chase Bank
- **Message content** from replies sent by household members to confirm or update budget categories

## How Data Is Used

All data collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

- Parsing bank transaction alerts and creating categorized entries in our household budgeting software (YNAB)
- Sending transaction notifications to household members via SMS/MMS
- Processing replies from household members to confirm or adjust transaction categories

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any personal data to third parties. Data is processed through the following services solely for the purpose of delivering notifications:

- **Twilio** — SMS/MMS delivery (subject to [Twilio's Privacy Policy](https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy))
- **YNAB (You Need A Budget)** — Budget management (subject to [YNAB's Privacy Policy](https://www.ynab.com/privacy-policy))
- **Anthropic** — AI-powered transaction categorization (subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy))

No data is used for marketing, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond household budget management.

## Data Storage and Security

- Transaction data and message content are processed in real time and stored only as needed for budget management
- Phone numbers are stored securely within the messaging platform configuration
- No data is stored in publicly accessible locations
- Access to all systems is restricted to the account owner

## Data Retention

- Transaction records are retained within YNAB as part of normal household budgeting
- SMS/MMS message logs are retained by Twilio per their standard retention policies
- No additional copies of message content are retained beyond what is necessary for processing

## Your Rights

As a household member participating in this service, you may at any time:

- **Opt out** of all messages by replying **STOP**
- **Request information** about what data is stored by contacting the account owner directly
- **Request deletion** of your phone number and any associated data

## Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Household members will be notified directly of any material changes.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the account owner directly via the household messaging group or at the phone number associated with this service.
