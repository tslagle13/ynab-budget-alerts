## Program Description

YNAB Budget Alerts is a private, household SMS/MMS notification service that automatically categorizes bank transactions and sends budget alerts to participating household members. When a transaction is detected on a linked bank account, the system sends a message with the merchant name, amount, and suggested budget category. Household members can reply to confirm, change, or manage the categorization.

This is a personal service operated for household budget management only. It is not a commercial product or marketing service.
